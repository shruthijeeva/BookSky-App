Verity

Collen Hoover

Verity by Colleen Hoover follows Lowen,a struggling writer hired to complete bestselling author Verity Crawford’s series. At Verity’s home, she discovers a chilling, unfinished autobiography revealing dark secrets about Verity’s past, including disturbing confessions about her marriage and motherhood. Torn between truth, lies, and love, Lowen faces haunting choices.
